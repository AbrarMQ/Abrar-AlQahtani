Flamingo

Flamingos usually stand on one leg while the other is tucked beneath their bodies. The reason for this behaviour is not fully understood.

  • Scientific Name: Phoenicopterus ruber.
  • Average Length: 3.3 - 4.6 feet tall.
  • Average Lifespan: 40 years.
  • Habitat: 6 species.

Flamingos are iconic pink birds with long legs and necks. Different species of flamingos are native to Central and South America, Africa, parts of Europe, and Asia.
